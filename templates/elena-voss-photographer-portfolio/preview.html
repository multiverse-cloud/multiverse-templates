About

The Eye Behind
the Lens

I'm Elena Voss, a documentary and fine-art photographer based in Berlin. For over fifteen years, I've chased the interplay of light and shadow across six continents, seeking the extraordinary within the ordinary.

My work has been featured in National Geographic, Vogue, and The New York Times. I believe photography is not about the camera — it's about the connection between the observer and the observed.

When I'm not behind a lens, you'll find me developing prints in my darkroom or wandering through flea markets searching for vintage cameras.
